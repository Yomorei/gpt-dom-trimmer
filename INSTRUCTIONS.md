## Install Tampermonkey :>

1) Install **[Tampermonkey](https://www.tampermonkey.net/)**

2) Click the Tampermonkey icon in your extensions bar -> **Create a new script**

3) Paste the contents of **`ChatGPT DOM Trimmer.js`** into the editor

4) Press **Ctrl + S** (saves), make sure the script is **enabled** lol.

5) Go to **[ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/)**

6) Look at the bottom right of your screen, you should see a very <sub>tiny</sub> **“Chat trim”** click it and enjoy XD.
